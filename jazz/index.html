Premium Experiences

For the ultimate game, you need the ultimate experience. The Utah Jazz offers three distinct choices when it comes to luxury entertainment, and all three are exciting and refined.

Luxury Suites

Welcome to the very best Utah Sports and Entertainment has to offer — Vivint Smart Home Arena Luxury Suites. Whether you're entertaining business clients, closing a major deal, or socializing with family and friends, a Luxury Suite provides all the convenience, comfort, and service you desire. Home to the Utah Jazz and the most popular musical acts and family shows, Vivint Smart Home Arena is the place to meet, greet, see, and be seen in Utah.

Your suite can enhance your corporate profile and give you an advantage over your competitors. It offers the chance to communicate your message in a luxurious setting while making entertaining as easy as it is elegant. Be a part of the most unique and exciting way to entertain in Utah by becoming a Vivint Smart Home Arena Luxury Suite holder.

Executives Club

The Interform Executives Club on the suite level is the most exclusive place to experience the excitement of the Utah Jazz, plus top performing acts and artists. This newly expanded area offers impeccable sightlines, in-seat service, and an exclusive VIP environment with an array of benefits. The Interform Executives Club is the only place to entertain top clients, business associates, and friends in Utah's greatest sports and entertainment facility!

  • Four seats in the Interform Executives Club to every Utah Jazz preseason and regular season home game
  • Complimentary full-service in-suite VIP dinner, including soft drinks at all Utah Jazz games (alcoholic beverages also available for purchase)
  • Incredible suite-level view
  • Private events with team coaches and management
  • Invitations to the annual Utah Jazz Hospitality Suite and Interform Executives Club Golf Tournament
  • Private club (21 and older only)
  • Reserved club parking
  • Business dress code
  • The first right to purchase Interform Executives Club seats or other premium tickets for all other Vivint Smart Home Arena events
  • Luxury dining area with table service and seat service
  • Large flat screen TVs located throughout the club

Lexus Club

One of the most prestigious clubs offered, the Lexus Courtside Club provides the ultimate in luxury and sophistication. Enjoy the the excitement of Jazz basketball from the comfort of oversized leather seats equipped with TV monitors — all in the front four rows of center court. You'll receive elite service and amenities, including pre-game meals and half-time snacks served in the privacy of the Lexus Club dining room.
